Travelling to Our Clinic

Home Pages Page Title

Have a hair transplant in Jakarta or Bali & we will help you with your travel arrangements

SPECIAL HOTELS OFFERS

We can assist you with booking another hotel of your choice or you can stay at our partner hotel at Taman Wisata Mangrove (PIK Jakarta Clinic)

PRIVATE TRANSFERS AND TRANSPORTATION

We arrange for transfers to and from the airport, and private shuttle services to and from the hotel to our clinic.

REFERRALS TO TOP DENTIST & PLASTIC SURGERY CLINICS

Apart from providing the best hair transplant technique in Jakarta or Bali, our team can connect you with other treatments such as dentistry, cosmetic and plastic surgery at Jakarta and Bali as well

Things To Do

Here is the things to do if you’re from another city or country

  • Book an online consultation and send your photos as per request for an initial assessment. Contact us.
  • Book a treatment date and follow the instruction about do and don’ts pre-op
  • Plan for at least 3 days of downtime afterward.
  • Ask us about our strong partnerships with leading internists, cosmetic surgeons, dentists, and dermatologists in Jakarta / Bali

Things To Avoid

Here is the things to do before doing hair transplant

  • Avoid long periods of exposure to sunlight or swimming trips for at least two weeks following your hair transplant.
  • Avoid smoking (including exposure to secondhand smoke), drinking alcohol, or conducting heavy exercise for one to two weeks

Upon Discharge, We will provide you with

Your necessary post-operative medications for 5 days.

A discharge letter and 100mL spray bottles for carry-on medication for patients with plans to fly

A letter to your doctor or other DHI clinic for follow-up (upon request).

Complete follow-up at regular intervals, done virtually or at the clinic.
